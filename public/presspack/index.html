Short biog

Ralph Wyld is a vibraphonist, percussionist, and composer, making his name playing original, contemporary music with artists such as Yazz Ahmed and Stan Sulzmann. Winner of both the Kenny Wheeler Jazz Prize, and the Dankworth Prize for Jazz Composition in 2015, he has performed at venues across the world, including gigs in the London, Berlin, and New York jazz festivals. His band, Mosaic, has played live on BBC Radio 3, and released an album on Edition Records described as an “outstanding debut” by MOJO. Ralph is a BlueHaus Mallets artist.
